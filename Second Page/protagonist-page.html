Kurosaki Ichigo

a brave and determined high school student with orange hair who has a strong sense of justice. He cares deeply about his friends and family, and will do whatever it takes to protect them.

Orihime Inoue

a kind and gentle classmate and friend of Ichigo who possesses a unique power that allows her to reject reality and manipulate matter. She is empathetic and caring towards others, but can also be quite sensitive.

Yasutora Sado (Chad)

a tall and quiet classmate of Ichigo who has a tough exterior but a kind heart. He is fiercely loyal to his friends and will not hesitate to stand up for what he believes is right.

Kuchiki Rukia

a strong-willed and dutiful female Soul Reaper. She has a dry sense of humor and can be quite serious at times, but also cares deeply about her friends.

Urahara Kisuke

He is a skilled inventor who is knowledgeable about the spiritual world, and he uses this expertise to create various devices and weapons to help himself and his allies in battle. Despite his laid-back and joking personality, Urahara is a reliable and valuable ally to those he considers his friends.

Ishida Uryu

an intelligent and analytical blue-haired boy who comes from a family of Quincy, a group of spirit hunters. He has a strong sense of justice. He can be quite reserved and is often focused on his own goals.
